Unicorn

A unicorn is a legendary creature that has been described since antiquity as a beast with a single large, pointed, spiraling horn projecting from its forehead. It is often associated with purity and grace.

Origins

The unicorn has been described in various cultures throughout history, including ancient Greek, Chinese, and Indian folklore. In European folklore, the unicorn is often depicted as a white horse-like or goat-like animal with a long horn and cloven hooves.

Characteristics

  • Single, spiraling horn on the forehead
  • White or pure-colored coat
  • Symbol of purity and grace
  • Often depicted as a horse-like or goat-like creature
  • Associated with healing and magical properties
